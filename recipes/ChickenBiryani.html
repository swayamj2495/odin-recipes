Chicken Biryani

Description :-

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant South Asian rice dish made with marinated chicken, basmati rice, and a blend of aromatic spices. It is layered and slow-cooked, creating a flavorful and rich one-pot meal.

Biryani has many regional variations: Hyderabadi, Lucknowi, and Kolkata styles, each with unique cooking techniques. The dish is often served at weddings, festivals, and family gatherings.

Ingredients :-

For the Chicken Marinade

  • Chicken (bone-in or boneless pieces)
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice

For the Rice

  • Basmati rice
  • Bay leaves
  • Cardamom pods
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Cloves
  • Salt

For the Garnish

  • Fried onions
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Mint leaves
  • Saffron milk (optional)

Recipe :-

  1. Marinate Chicken
    • Mix chicken with yogurt, spices, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice.
    • Rest for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook Rice
    • Boil basmati rice with whole spices until 70% cooked.
    • Drain and set aside.
  3. Cook Chicken
    • Sauté onions until golden, then add marinated chicken.
    • Cook until partially done.
  4. Layer Biryani
    • In a pot, layer chicken at the bottom, then rice.
    • Top with fried onions, coriander, mint, and saffron milk.
  5. Dum Cooking
    • Seal the pot with a lid or dough.
    • Cook on low heat for 20–25 minutes until flavors infuse.
  6. Serve
    • Fluff gently and serve hot with raita or salad.
